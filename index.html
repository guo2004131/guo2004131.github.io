Brief Bio

I have joined PAII Inc. (Ping An AI Institute), Bethesda, as a senior researcher since 2019. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, University of South Carolina. My advisor is Dr. Song Wang. By that time, I worked closely with Dr. Julius Fridriksson on Aphasia studies and brain imaging.


My research interest lies broadly in computer vision, medical imaging, and machine learning. More specifically, I am focusing on the automated segmentation in cancer radiothearpy planning, including esophageal gross tumor volume segmentation (GTV), clinical target volume segmentation (CTV), chest and head & neck organ at risk parsing (OAR), and lymph node delineation (LN).
