Brief Bio

I have been a senior researcher at PAII Inc. since 2019. My current projects are focusing on the automated segmentation in cancer radiotherapy planning, including esophageal gross tumor volume segmentation (GTV), clinical target volume segmentation (CTV), chest and head & neck organ at risk parsing (OAR), and lymph node delineation (LN). I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, University of South Carolina. My research interest lies broadly in computer vision, medical imaging, and machine learning.
